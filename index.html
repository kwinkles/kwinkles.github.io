Let's make a hardware.

I am writing this blog to explore a bit with some hardware verification technologies that I don't yet know. The plan is to design some basic hardware in Verilog, then test it with SystemVerilog and UVM. I have some experience with OVM, but it's not that in-depth, and since OVM is not being updated as of version 2.1.2, UVM - which is based on OVM - is the next logical step. The hardware won't be complicated, as I am mostly trying to learn the ins and outs of UVM and the OOP features it provides.
